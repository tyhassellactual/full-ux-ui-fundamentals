Making High-Performers Top-Performers

Once you have the right people, those that are high performers that fit your culture, and have promise to grow and increase your business, what do you do with them? The answer isn't to simply fill a position, say good luck, and hope for the best. Having a plan, program, and environment of professional and personal growth and stewardship is vital to creating and industry leading pipe hitter.



Professional Growth: Lead, Coach, Mentor

  • Onboarding (Program introduction & Assessment):
    • Define objectives and current outcomes of current role
    • Conduct a skills gap analysis and a career goals assessment
    • Identify key participants and stakeholders (leaders, coaches, mentors, employees)
  • Leading (Building Leadership & Stretegic Thinking):
    • Workshops and Training: Leadership development, decision making, strategic thinking
    • Action Learning: Assigning leadership and associate leadership roles in projects
    • Feedback and Evaluation: Holistic assessments and evaluations to develop feedback loop and understanding
  • Coaching (Enhancing Skills & Performance):
    • Skill-Based Training: Industry-specific upskilling of technical and soft-skills
    • One-on-One Coaching: Personalized career and performance coaching dedicated to developing ownership and continuity within the team
    • Performance Tracking: Set and measure progress with individualized key-performance-indicators (KPIs)
  • Mentoring (Long-Term Growth & Succession Planning):
    • Mentor-Mentee Matching: Pair experienced professionals with junior and developing talent
    • Knowledge Transfer Initiatives: Shadowing, cross-funtional training, product defined inter-department meetups
    • Career Progression Plans: Define pathways for promotions, role expansion, and career growth
  • Programming Integration & Ongoing Support:
    • Networking and Collaboration: Workplave groups and indutry events
    • Continuous Learning: Planned microlearning modules, certification programs, and e-learning platforms
    • Review and Adaptation: Regular feedback loops to improve and update programs and strategies based on outcomes and company needs
